Home Page

Hello! Welcome to my little homepage! This is mostly meant to be a blog for me to talk about things I'm passionate about, as well as maybe a place to collect buttons, be a part of webrings, etc..

One of my favorite things about this, and about the indie web in general, is that I do not have to adhere to strict "modern web ux standards". I want to focus on stuff like accessibility and a little bit of creativity, without all of the animations, content jumping around, javascript-enabled-only, and other modern problems..
